# Overview — kb_youtube

The entry point and evolving synthesis for this knowledge base. As videos accumulate, this page should grow into a map of the territory: the big recurring themes, the main debates, and where to start.

## What this is
A personal knowledge base built from YouTube videos, weighted toward **tech/AI** and **ideas/commentary**. Each video becomes a [[index|source page]]; ideas get synthesized into concept pages that compound over time.

## How to navigate
- **[[index]]** — full catalog of pages.
- **Concepts** — the synthesis layer; start here for "what do I know about X?".
- **Channels / People** — who said what.
- **Analyses** — answers to questions I've asked, filed for reuse.

## Themes
*(1 source so far — the full Jensen Huang × CNA interview.)*
- **AI as an industry, not a model** — the [[ai-five-layer-cake]] (energy → chips → infrastructure → models → applications) and [[agentic-ai]] are the core mental models seeded so far.
- **AI & the labor market** — [[ai-and-jobs]]: the optimistic "you'll lose your job to someone who learned AI" + "more jobs in 5 years" thesis. Currently one-sided; needs an opposing view ingested.
- **AI geopolitics** — [[ai-us-china-competition]]: rivalry, export-control blowback, and the compete-vs-cooperate question; [[tsmc|Taiwan]] as the manufacturing fulcrum.
- **Leadership & the human edge** — [[leadership-in-the-age-of-ai]] and [[intelligence-as-a-commodity]]: when cognition is cheap, character/ambition/empathy and the ability to empower others are what differentiate.
