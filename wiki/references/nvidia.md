# Nvidia

AI chip and computing company founded and led by [[jensen-huang]]. Occupies the **chips** layer of the [[ai-five-layer-cake]] but frames its product as "a factory / infrastructure for the world," not just a tool.

## Why it comes up
- The subject's company in [[jensen-huang-cna-interview]]. Stated as a multi-hundred-billion-dollar company growing ~100%/yr, spending most of its AI-infrastructure capex in [[tsmc|Taiwan]]. Huang calls it "the world's smallest large company."
- **Next-gen platforms:** Grace Blackwell and Vera Rubin together imply ~**$1 trillion of sales** over the coming years → hundreds of billions flowing to Taiwan ("the highest ever in history").
- Central to the [[ai-us-china-competition|US–China]] export-control discussion (the "vacuum" filled by Huawei) and to Taiwan's supply chain.
- Culture: claims best-in-world retention (33-year employees); both of Huang's children work there. See [[leadership-in-the-age-of-ai]].
