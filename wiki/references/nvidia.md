# Nvidia

AI chip and computing company founded and led by [[jensen-huang]]. Occupies the **chips** layer of the [[ai-five-layer-cake]] but frames its product as "a factory / infrastructure for the world," not just a tool.

## Why it comes up
- The subject's company in [[jensen-huang-cna-interview]]. Stated as a multi-hundred-billion-dollar company growing ~100%/yr, spending most of its AI-infrastructure capex in [[tsmc|Taiwan]].
- Central to the [[ai-us-china-competition|US–China]] export-control discussion (the "vacuum" filled by Huawei) and to Taiwan's supply chain.
