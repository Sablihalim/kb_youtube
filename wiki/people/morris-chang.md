# Morris Chang

Founder of [[tsmc|TSMC]]. In [[jensen-huang-cna-interview]], [[jensen-huang]] describes him as a close friend and formative supporter.

## As described by Jensen Huang
- "Without TSMC and Morris, Nvidia wouldn't be here today." Morris/TSMC supported Nvidia from when it was small, taking risk together; their histories are "highly intertwined."
- On his first return to Taiwan after leaving at age 5, Jensen came back to see Morris.
- Personal: dined together the night before the interview (with Jensen's parents, wife Lori, and Madison); Morris is "an avid reader" who shares book summaries; they enjoy whiskey together.
