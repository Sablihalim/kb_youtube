# Ilya Sutskever

Deep-learning researcher; co-founder and former chief scientist of [[openai|OpenAI]]. A former student of [[geoffrey-hinton]] (part of the AlexNet work that made object recognition succeed). Discussed in [[hinton-diary-of-a-ceo]].

## As described by Geoffrey Hinton
- "Very important in AlexNet" and "the main force behind things like GPT-2, which then led to [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]]."
- Left [[openai|OpenAI]] over **safety** concerns; Hinton finds this "haunting" — "he knows something I don't know about what might happen next."
- Investors backed his new venture (Safe Superintelligence Inc.) with billions "anyway, because they have so much faith in Ilya" — which Hinton calls a reasonable bet.
- His exit is a key data point in Hinton's [[ai-existential-risk]] argument.
