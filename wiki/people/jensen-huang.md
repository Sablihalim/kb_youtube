# Jensen Huang

Founder and CEO of [[nvidia|Nvidia]]; the central subject of [[jensen-huang-cna-interview]] (CNA, 2026).

## Positions & views (as they appear across sources)
- **AI is a new industry, not just a model** — articulates the [[ai-five-layer-cake]] framework.
- **Contrarian on AI job loss** — "you won't lose your job to AI, but to someone who learned AI"; calls the layoff narrative "too lazy." See [[ai-and-jobs]].
- **Compete *and* cooperate with China** — China is "everybody's greatest rival," but two separate AI ecosystems would be "not wise." See [[ai-us-china-competition]].
- **Taiwan-centric view of the AI supply chain** — close ties to [[tsmc]] and the island's manufacturing base.
- **Work ethic / leadership** — works seven days a week; "I hope to die on the job." *(Detail from the post-33:00 portion of the interview — not yet fully ingested.)*

## Biographical notes
- Longest-tenured big-tech CEO; built Nvidia into a multi-hundred-billion-dollar company growing ~100%/yr (as stated in the interview).
- Joined President Trump's 2026 Beijing trip last-minute (boarded Air Force One in Alaska).
